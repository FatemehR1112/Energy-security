Overview

This repository contains the scenario definitions, input datasets, and supporting materials associated with the study:

"Assessing Energy Security Risks to Ireland’s Net-Zero Transition Under Cross-Border Supply Uncertainty".

The study investigates how geopolitical events, international supply-chain disruptions, and price volatility in imported energy commodities and critical minerals affect the resilience of Ireland's least-cost pathway to net-zero emissions. A novel uncertainty framework is developed by integrating Monte Carlo simulations with the TIMES-Ireland (TIM) energy system model to assess the impacts of cross-border risks on energy security, system costs, technology deployment, and greenhouse gas emissions.
